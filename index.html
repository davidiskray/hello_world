Philosophy

Capital Financial is an employee benefits consulting firm dedicated to providing exceptional service to Arizona school districts. Our staff specializes in developing benefits programs that fit any given budget while ensuring district employees are satisfied.

Employee benefits often are a large part of a school district’s budgeting plan, so we understand the importance of establishing a cost-effective program that meets your specific needs. We have worked with districts with as little as 25 employees to districts with more than 1,500. No matter the size, the goal is always the same: provide exceptional service so you and your employees can have peace of mind.

Our consultation services include:

• Procurement and product evaluation
• Fully insured and alternate-funded medical programs
• Ancillary and worksite benefit programs
• Employee communication strategies and engagement
• Commercial and Workers’ Compensation programs

Capital Financial is as an independent consulting firm, allowing us to be unbiased and uninfluenced in our recommendations to our clients. We provide value for our clients by working with only the highest-rated companies in the employee benefits market.

Experience

The partners at Capital Financial have worked with more than 170 Arizona school districts. Collectively, our young team has decades of experience in the industry, and we use our knowledge to customize benefits programs to any given district’s specific needs.

Employee satisfaction in the school industry often depends on these programs more so than other markets, so our valuable experience puts us in a position to meet our clients’ unique expectations. More importantly, we stay up to date with the latest trends and offer fresh insight and strategy to develop effective benefits programs.

Research

The healthcare and benefits industries are constantly evolving. The passing of The Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (PPACA), or Obamacare, was a historical event in the United States, and it’s critical for employers to be aware of how they’re affected. Now more than ever, it’s important to work with a knowledgeable consultant. Capital Financial works with Arizona school districts to ensure they’re aware of their new responsibilities and the penalties for not complying with policy changes.

Our education directly affects our clients, so we know how important it is to keep up with the latest trends. We perform research through seminars, newsletters and periodicals from industry leaders. In doing so, we’re in a position to tailor benefits programs that’ll fit your budget and give your employees the confidence they want in regards to healthcare.
